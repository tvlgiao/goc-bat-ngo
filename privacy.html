Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Góc Bất Ngờ ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects minimal information necessary for operation:

  • TikTok Account Information: When you authorize our app via TikTok Login Kit, we receive your TikTok user ID and basic profile information to publish content to your account.
  • OAuth Tokens: Access and refresh tokens are stored locally on your device to maintain your session.

3. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate and publish video content to authorized TikTok accounts.
  • To manage content posting schedules.
  • We do not use your information for advertising or share it with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All authentication tokens and credentials are stored locally on the user's device. We do not store any user data on external servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. We only interact with TikTok's API to publish content as authorized by you.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained locally until you revoke access or delete them. You can revoke our app's access at any time through your TikTok account settings.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. OAuth tokens are stored securely on your local device and transmitted only over encrypted HTTPS connections.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via our YouTube channel.
